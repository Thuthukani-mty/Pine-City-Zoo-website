INSECT HOUSE

Discover the fascinating world of insects at our Insect House! Home to a diverse collection of creepy crawlies, this exhibit offers an up-close look at some of the most intriguing and important creatures on the planet.

From colorful butterflies and moths to industrious ants and beetles, our Insect House showcases the incredible diversity and adaptability of insects. Learn about their life cycles, behaviors, and the vital roles they play in ecosystems around the world.

At Pine City Zoo, we are committed to educating our visitors about the importance of insects and their conservation. Our Insect House provides a safe and enriching environment for these fascinating creatures, allowing visitors to observe them in habitats that mimic their natural environments.

Come visit our Insect House and experience the wonder of these tiny yet mighty animals up close. Our knowledgeable staff are always available to answer any questions you may have and provide insights into the captivating world of insects.
