LOST FOREST

Escape to the tranquil Lost Forest, a serene area within the zoo where you can relax and enjoy a picnic under the shade of towering trees by the picturesque lake. The Lost Forest offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the zoo, allowing you to connect with nature and unwind in a beautiful setting.

Bring your own picnic or grab a meal from one of our nearby eateries and savor it while taking in the sights and sounds of the forest. The area is perfect for families, couples, or solo visitors looking for a quiet spot to relax and recharge.

Explore the walking trails that wind through the forest, offering opportunities to spot local wildlife and enjoy the natural beauty of the area. Whether you're looking to read a book, meditate, or simply enjoy a leisurely stroll, the Lost Forest is the ideal destination.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

You will find it in the central part of the zoo, near the amphitheatre and the lake.
