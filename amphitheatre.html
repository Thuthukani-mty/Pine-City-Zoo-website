AMPHITHEATRE

Our amphitheatre hosts a variety of educational and entertaining events throughout the year. From wildlife presentations to musical performances, there's always something exciting happening here. The amphitheatre is designed to provide a comfortable and engaging environment for visitors of all ages.

Check our event schedule to see what's coming up next and plan your visit accordingly. Whether you're interested in learning more about our animal residents or simply looking for a fun outing with family and friends, the amphitheatre is the perfect place to be.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The amphitheatre is located near the main entrance, adjacent to the Lost Forest area. Look for the large open-air structure with tiered seating and a stage.
