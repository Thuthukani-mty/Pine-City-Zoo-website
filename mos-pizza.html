MO'S PIZZA

Craving a delicious pizza or pasta dish while exploring the zoo? Look no further than Mo's Pizza, our family-friendly eatery that offers a variety of mouth-watering options to satisfy your hunger.

At Mo's Pizza, we pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients to create our signature pizzas and pasta dishes. From classic margherita to meat lover's, our pizzas are made to order and cooked to perfection in our stone oven. We also offer a range of pasta dishes, including spaghetti bolognese, fettuccine alfredo, and penne arrabbiata, all served with a side of garlic bread.

In addition to our delicious food, Mo's Pizza also offers a welcoming atmosphere for families and groups. Our friendly staff are dedicated to providing excellent service and ensuring that your dining experience is enjoyable.
