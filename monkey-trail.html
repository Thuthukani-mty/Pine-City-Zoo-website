MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is an exciting and adventurous obstacle course designed for visitors of all ages to enjoy. This thrilling attraction allows you to navigate through a series of challenging obstacles, including rope bridges, climbing walls, and zip lines, all while being surrounded by the lush greenery of the zoo.

At Pine City Zoo, we are committed to providing fun and engaging experiences for our visitors. The Monkey Trail is a perfect way to combine physical activity with the joy of exploring nature. Whether you're looking for a fun family outing or an adrenaline-pumping adventure, the Monkey Trail has something for everyone.

Come and test your agility and courage on the Monkey Trail! Our friendly staff are always available to ensure your safety and provide assistance as needed. Don't forget to wear comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes for the best experience.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

You will find it at the Adventure Zone, near the entrance of the zoo.
